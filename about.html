About Us

Facilities

We serve professional builders and do-it-yourself homeowners. We stock hardware, paint, lawn and garden supplies, plumbing and electrical supplies at our locations. Our customers appreciate that we stock premium grade lumber, not found in all lumber yards, and everything else you need to build a house, or renovate and repair one. Our yard and store in Siler City is located at 510 S. Chatham Avenue.

National Buying Clout

The Belk name is known through out the southeast. Although we are not a part of the department store business, we come from the same family that established that company and a 200,000 square foot distribution facility in Monroe, North Carolina, stocking 30,000 products from 500 manufacturers. In addition to supplying Belk Building Supply, that location serves 3,000 retail stores in the southeast. Affiliated with Distribution America, a $2 billion national purchasing cooperative, Belk Building Supply is able to buy products at the same low prices other national chains pay, and of course, we pass that savings along to you.
